Restaurant Description

Delicious Cafe is a comfortable, stylish eatery with a focus on home-cooked food and tasty dishes. Started in 1942, Delicious Cafe has made a name for itself with a legacy of great customer service and fine, delicious meals with an affordable price tag. Our mission statement (inspired by the life motto of our founder) is:"Good food and good family for the every man".If that sounds good to you, then come on down to Delicious Cafe for a good time. Next time you're in the area, come on by for food worth raving about. We offer catering as well, so book us for your next party!
